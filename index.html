Frequently Asked Questions

Can Smart Food replace all my meals?

+

Smart Food products are designed to provide complete nutrition and can replace any meal. However, we recommend a balanced approach with a mix of traditional food and Smart Food products.

+

Yes, all our products are vegetarian. Many are also vegan - check individual product details for specific information.

How long do Smart Food products last?

+

Unopened products typically have a shelf life of 12 months. Please check individual product packaging for specific expiration dates.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.
